MyCare TVaaP

HCA Healthcare wanted to create a TV platform that would combine entertainment and clinical information related to their care.

To achieve this, my team and I collaborated with IT leadership and clinical staff at TriStar Hendersonville Medical Center to create a platform that places patient data on the same panel as entertainment content with added Apple TV features. Additionally, this platform allows physicians to communicate critical information with patients at each step of their care.
