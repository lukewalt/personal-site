Neutron

Neutron is a design system that is built to scale to meet the needs of teams and brands across the HCA Healthcare enterprise. The intention for this tool is to ensure consistency of design and UI across all products while also creating internal resources and tools for development teams enterprise

The theory and framework of Neutron is derived from Brad Frost's Atomic Design which creates the ability to create systems that promote consistency and scalability while simultaneously showing things in their final context.

In collaboration with Enterprise Architecture and the internal Cloud team, I worked to create the basis for which the system would be hosted, distribued, and consumed, as well as a contribution model developed through a partnership with the HCA Healthcare inner-sourcing board.

MyCare TVaaP

HCA Healthcare wanted to create a TV platform that would combine entertainment and clinical information related to their care.

To achieve this, my team and I collaborated with IT leadership and clinical staff at TriStar Hendersonville Medical Center to create a platform that places patient data on the same panel as entertainment content with added Apple TV features. Additionally, this platform allows physicians to communicate critical information with patients at each step of their care.
